Analysis of Obesity Rates in Europe

  1. Eastern Europe (red line) has the highest increase in obesity rates, starting from around 20% in 2014 and reaching over 30% by 2022.
  2. Southern Europe (orange line) also shows a notable increase, beginning at approximately 18% in 2014 and rising to over 26% in 2022.
  3. Central Europe (purple line) starts at just above 16% in 2014, showing moderate growth and stabilizing at around 22% by 2022.
  4. Northern Europe (green line) shows a gradual increase from around 17% in 2014 to just under 20% by 2022.
  5. Western Europe (blue line) shows the smallest growth in obesity rates, starting at approximately 17% in 2014 and reaching about 18% by 2022.
