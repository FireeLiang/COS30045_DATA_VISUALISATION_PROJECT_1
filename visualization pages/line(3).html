Analysis of Obesity Rates in Europe

Female Population

  1. Eastern Europe (red line) has the highest share of obesity, rising steadily from approximately 24% in 2014 to around 28% by 2022.
  2. Southern Europe (purple line) also shows a significant increase, from about 18% in 2014 to over 22% in 2022.
  3. Northern Europe (green line), Central Europe (orange line), and Western Europe (blue line) have similar trends, starting around 18% in 2014 and leveling off at approximately 21% by 2022.
  4. Western Europe has the lowest increase in obesity rates compared to other regions, showing a relatively smaller change over time.
